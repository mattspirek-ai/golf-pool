How It Works

Pick 2 golfers from each of the 6 tiers. Tiers are ranked by odds — Tier 1 is the favorites, Tier 6 is the rest of the field.
Scoring: Keep the lowest score between your two golfers per tier. Drop your worst-performing tier entirely. Your final score = sum of best 5 tier scores.
Missed Cut / WD: Receives the highest individual round score from the field for Saturday and Sunday.
Winner Bonus: Pick the tournament champion correctly = −10 points.
Tiebreaker 1: Closest predicted winning score vs. par (e.g., −15).
Tiebreaker 2: Most players making the cut out of your 12.
Payout: 1st = 65% · 2nd = 25% · 3rd = 10%.
